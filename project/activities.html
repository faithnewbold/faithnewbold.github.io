Activities

Walt Disney World
Walt Disney World is one of the funnest activities located in Central Florida! It consists of multiple resorts and shopping centers, as well as multiple theme parks! The theme parts consist of Magic Kingdom, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom, Epcot, and two water parks, Typhoon Lagoon and Blizzard Beach! There is something fun for people of all ages at Walt Disney World Resort!
To learn more about pricing and specifics, check out their website: Walt Disney World Resort
Universal Studios Orlando
Universal Studios Orlando is also an amusement park that offers fun for all ages! With themed parks such as The Wizarding World of Harry Potter and Jurassic Park, visiting Universal Studios is something that you won't want to miss!
To learn more about pricing and specifics, check out their website: Universal Studios Orlando
Wild Florida
Wild Florida is a different type of once in a lifetime experience! There you will be able to see some of Florida's unique wildlife, embark on airboat rides through Florida's wetlands, and even have the unique opportunity to hold a baby alligator!
To learn more, check out their website: Wild Florida
Daytona Speedway
At Daytona International Speedway, you will have the opportunity to view some of the best racing cars, attend a race, or even take a tour of the facility and get the unique experience to go down and stand on the track!
To learn more about pricing and specifics, check out their website: Daytona Speedway
East Coast Beaches
The beautiful state of Florida also is home to many beautiful beaches if you are wanting to take a quick swim, get a tan, or even try surfing! Some of the most popular beaches near central Florida include Cocoa Beach, New Smyrna Beach, Daytona Beach, and Vero Beach!
