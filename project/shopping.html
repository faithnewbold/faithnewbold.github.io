Shopping

Florida offers many amazing opportunitites for shopping. Disney Springs offers a unique experience, as it includes many shops, integrated with the Disney experience! The Florida Mall offers a typical mall like experience if that is what you are looking for! Or, if you are looking for something unique to the coast, check out Ron Jon Surf Shop, where you can find surf supplies, and plenty of souvenir items!
