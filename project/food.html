Yellow Dog Eats
Yellow Dog Eats is one of our favorite locally owned restaurants, with 2 locations in Gotha, Florida and New Smyrna Beach, Florida! They are known for their delicious pulled pork, and it is definitely something you won't want to miss!

Long Doggers
Long Doggers is another amazing restaurant specific to Florida! With multiple locations along the East Coast, Long Doggers has something for everyone, but they are known mainly for their fresh seafood!
